🇬🇧 5 Days London Classic: The Royal Experience

Duration: 4 Nights / 5 Days

Theme: History, Culture & Luxury

Base Price: Starting from ₹1,25,000* per person (Excl. Flights)

📍 Itinerary Overview (The Base Plan)

  • Day 1: Welcome to the City of Dreams
    • Arrival at London Heathrow.
    • Private Transfer to your Hotel in Central London.
    • Evening: A relaxing Thames River Evening Cruise with views of the Tower Bridge.
  • Day 2: Royal Heritage & Icons
    • Morning: Guided tour of Buckingham Palace (Changing of the Guard).
    • Afternoon: Visit Westminster Abbey and photo-stop at Big Ben.
    • Evening: Entry to The London Eye for a bird’s-eye view of the city.
  • Day 3: History & Mystery
    • Morning: Explore the Tower of London and see the Crown Jewels.
    • Afternoon: Visit the British Museum (World’s largest collection of history).
    • Evening: Free time for shopping at Oxford Street.
  • Day 4: Day Trip to Windsor or Oxford
    • Full-day excursion to Windsor Castle (The King’s residence) OR a trip to Oxford University City.
    • Return to London by evening.
  • Day 5: Farewell London
    • Last-minute souvenir shopping at Covent Garden.
    • Private transfer to Heathrow Airport for your flight back home.

✅ What’s Included (Inclusions)

  • 4 Nights accommodation in a 4-Star Hotel (Daily Breakfast).
  • Airport Pick-up & Drop via Private Luxury Sedan.
  • All Sightseeing entry tickets mentioned in the itinerary.
  • Standard London Underground (Tube) Pass for local travel.

🇬🇷 Title: The Grand Greece Odyssey (Classic History & Island Luxury)

Duration: 7 Days / 6 Nights

Destinations: Athens (2N) → Mykonos (2N) → Santorini (2N)

1. Detailed Itinerary

Private transfer to Santorini Airport for your flight back to Athens or onward connection.

Day 1: Arrival in the Land of Gods (Athens)

Private transfer from Athens International Airport to your hotel.

Evening: Leisure walk through Plaka, the historic neighborhood at the foot of the Acropolis.

Day 2: The Cradle of Western Civilization

Morning: Guided tour of the Acropolis (Parthenon, Erechtheion, and Propylaea).

Afternoon: Visit the Panathenaic Stadium (site of the first modern Olympic Games) and the Temple of Olympian Zeus.

Day 3: Voyage to the Glamorous Mykonos

High-speed ferry transfer from Piraeus Port to Mykonos.

Check-in at a boutique hotel and sunset cocktails at Little Venice.

Day 4: Mykonos – Sun, Sand, and Windmills

Free day to explore the iconic whitewashed streets or relax at Psarou Beach.

Optional: Evening tour to the sacred island of Delos (a UNESCO World Heritage site).

Day 5: The Volcanic Beauty of Santorini

Ferry transfer to Santorini, the most famous volcanic island in the world.

Check-in and evening dinner overlooking the Caldera.

Day 6: Oia – The World’s Most Famous Sunset

Morning: Visit the Red Beach and the prehistoric ruins of Akrotiri.

Evening: Walk the marble paths of Oia village to witness the legendary blue-domed churches and the sunset.

Day 7: Farewell to the Aegean
